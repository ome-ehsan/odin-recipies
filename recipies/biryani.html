Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish, perfect for festive occasions or a special family meal. This dish features tender pieces of chicken cooked with fragrant basmati rice, spices, and herbs.

Serve it hot for a delightful treat that combines spicy chicken, aromatic rice, and a medley of spices for an unforgettable taste experience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 pound chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnishing.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste to the remaining onions in the pot and saut√© for a few minutes. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the chicken is done and the oil separates from the masala.
  5. In a separate pot, boil water and cook the soaked rice until it is 70% cooked. Drain the water and set the rice aside.
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken masala in the pot. Sprinkle saffron milk, fresh coriander, and mint leaves on top. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Once done, gently mix the biryani and serve hot, garnished with the fried onions.
